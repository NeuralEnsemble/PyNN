=========
Downloads
=========

Source distributions
--------------------

The latest alpha version of PyNN (0.8alpha1) may be downloaded from the `INCF Software Centre`_.
This is currently experimental, and should probably only be used by experienced users of PyNN.

The `latest stable version of PyNN`_ (0.7.5) may be downloaded from the
`Python Package Index`_ or from the `INCF Software Centre`_.
This is recommended for most users.
